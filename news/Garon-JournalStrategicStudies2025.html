10 December 2025

Professor Garon has published a new article in The Journal of Strategic Studies entitled “Toward a Global History of Bombing in the Second World War: Taking Japan Seriously.” The publication revises key arguments from Richard Overy’s Rain of Ruin, a new work on the subject that offers a critical reassessment of the bombing of Japan. By placing Japan more fully within a global history of aerial warfare, the article contributes to a deeper understanding of strategic bombing during the Second World War.
