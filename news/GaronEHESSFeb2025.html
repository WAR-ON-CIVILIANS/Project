Upcoming Talk in Paris of Professor Garon

Sheldon Garon, Principal Investigator in "The Global War on Civilians" project, will give a talk entitled “The Transnational Destruction of Cities in Europe and East Asia, 1914–1945” as part of the Environmental History Seminar at EHESS.

The lecture explores strategies to attack and defend cities from a transnational and comparative perspective.

The talk will take place on Friday, 6 February, from 10:00 to 12:00, at 54 Boulevard Raspail, Room AS1 24 (Paris), as part of the Environmental History Seminar of the Groupe de recherche en histoire environnementale (GRHEN) at EHESS. This research is conducted within the framework of the ERC Advanced Grant project, “The Global War on Civilians, 1905–1945,” hosted by the Centre d’histoire de Sciences Po (Paris).

The seminar is open to the public, and no registration is required.
