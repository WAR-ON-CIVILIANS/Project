3 October, 2025

Professor Garon delivered a seminar at Tokyo College.

On October 3, 2025, Professor Sheldon Garon (Princeton University & Sciences Po) delivered a seminar at Tokyo College titled “The Global War on Civilians: Applying Transnational History in a Collaborative Project.” The talk introduced ongoing collaborative research that examines civilian suffering and state policies across multiple nations, highlighting the value of a transnational approach to understanding global conflicts.
